About test_page

test_page is a technology studio founded in 2015 with headquarters in Bogotá and remote teams across Latin America and Europe. We specialize in web development, UX/UI design, and AI-based solutions. Our goal is to make technology accessible, efficient, and human-centered.

We’ve collaborated with startups, NGOs, and Fortune 500 companies, delivering products that serve millions of users globally. We believe that innovation happens when strategy, creativity, and engineering align.

Our Core Values
